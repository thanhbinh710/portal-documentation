Create an app

Follow these steps to create your first application

  1. Click on CREATE on the developer home screen
  2. Enter your app name (e.g. Testingapp)
  3. Select a team to deploy your app (e.g. guest)
  4. Provide a short description of your app (e.g. My first app)
  5. Agree with our Terms of use and click CREATE

At the end of creation, you can see your app on the developer home screen
